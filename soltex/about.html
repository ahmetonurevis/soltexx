about us

First of all, we are a family company. All family members have been in the textile business since 1960. Currently the 3rd generation manages the company. The 4th generation has already started to develop new products. The center of textile in Turkey is the city of Bursa. Bursa is an ancient city and has been producing textile products for exactly 1400 years. In the light of the ancient knowledge coming from this tradition, we produce in these lands. We carry out all operations under full control, from yarn production to fabric weaving and fabric dyeing. We take the production of recycled raw materials very seriously. We renew our recycling certificates every year. And we do much more than certification in this regard. For a more livable world, we recycle all petrochemical products in the production phase. We produce fireproof fabric at international standards. Apart from plain fabric production, we also produce fancy fabric. So in the end, we;

We produce fabric from past to future.
